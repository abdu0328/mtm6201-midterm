About

Our Origin Story

Magic Coffee Co. began in 2022, and have slowly been establishing ourselves in the community in the past year. The idea first started with our co founders getting bored of their morning coffee fixtures, and decided to have fun with creating wacky creations...

Vision

We aim to experiment with new coffee bean blends, flavours, and brewing methods to create innovative and unforgettable coffee experiences for our customers.

Magic Roasters

Experience the magic of Magic Coffee Co.'s roasters, who conjure captivating flavors and aromas with their expert skills. From carefully selected beans, they create enchanting blends that range from rich and velvety dark roasts to vibrant and nuanced light roasts. Prepare to be spellbound by the bewitching flavors awaiting you at Magic Coffee Co., where our roasters' expertise brings your coffee dreams to life.
